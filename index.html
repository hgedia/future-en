Who We Help

You are welcome here

We support anyone in need in our area, including:

Refugees and asylum seekers
Undocumented migrants
Congolese and Franco-African communities
Black and Minority Ethnic (BME) people
Romanian, Polish & Greek communities
People feeling lonely or isolated
Young people needing guidance

We cover: Haringey, Hackney, Tower Hamlets, Newham, Waltham Forest and Islington.

What We Do

We are here to help

We offer free, friendly support to people who need it most — regardless of their background or where they come from.

Advice & Guidance

We help with benefits, housing, jobs, and knowing your rights — in a language you understand.

Mental Health Support

We connect people to counselling and mental health services, and run supportive group sessions.

Training Workshops

We run workshops to build skills, confidence, and knowledge so people can improve their lives.

Youth Support

We help young people with education, training, and personal development so they can reach their potential.

Community Events

We bring people together through regular meetings and events so no one feels alone.

Legal Referrals

We help connect people with free legal advice for immigration and other challenges.
