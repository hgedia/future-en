Donate to Future Enlightenment

Your gift — large or small — helps us continue our vital work supporting vulnerable people in North London.

Why Donate

Your money goes directly to helping people

Every pound you donate helps us:

  • ✓ Run advice sessions and drop-ins
  • ✓ Pay for venue hire and session co-ordinators
  • ✓ Cover volunteer expenses
  • ✓ Provide training workshops
  • ✓ Create a safe, welcoming space for vulnerable people
"Your donation will directly support a dedicated venue to serve immigrants, asylum seekers, and migrants in distress — including Romanian, Polish, and Greek communities. Every contribution makes a meaningful impact."

— Alain Musa, Co-ordinator

Online Donations Temporarily Suspended

Please note: We have temporarily suspended online donations while we put stronger safeguards in place. We want to make sure every contribution reaches us safely and securely.

If you would like to support our work in the meantime, please get in touch with us directly and we will advise you on how to donate safely. Thank you for your patience and understanding.
