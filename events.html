Regular Sessions

Our Fortnightly Meetings

We hold regular drop-in sessions, workshops, and youth sessions every other Thursday. All are welcome — no appointment needed for drop-ins.

01

Drop-in Session

When
Every other Thursday
Time
2:00 pm – 7:00 pm
Where
Marcus Garvey Library,
1 Philip Lane, London, N15 4JA
(First floor)

Come for advice, a friendly chat, sharing experiences or to find out about our services. No booking needed.

02

Workshops

When
Every other Thursday (depending on demand)
Time
2:00 pm – 7:00 pm
Where
Marcus Garvey Library, 1 Philip Lane, London, N15 4JA

Skills and training sessions. Topics vary — contact us to find out what's on.

03

Youth Sessions

When
Every other Thursday (depending on demand)
Time
2:00 pm – 7:00 pm
Where
Marcus Garvey Library, 1 Philip Lane, London, N15 4JA

Support for young people: education, skills, life guidance. Open to ages 16–25.

i
Good to know: All our sessions are free. We speak English, French, Lingala and other languages. If you need a translator or have accessibility needs, please get in touch before you visit.
