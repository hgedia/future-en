Events & Meetings

Come and meet us. Our sessions are free, friendly and open to everyone.

Regular Sessions

Our Weekly Meetings

We hold regular drop-in sessions and workshops. All are welcome — no appointment needed for drop-ins.

Drop-in Session

When: Every Tuesday & Thursday
Time: 10:00 am – 1:00 pm
Where: Tottenham Green, Haringey, London

Come for advice, a friendly chat, or to find out about our services. No booking needed.

Community Workshops

When: Every Saturday (monthly)
Time: 11:00 am – 2:00 pm
Where: Tottenham Green, Haringey, London

Skills and training sessions. Topics change each month — contact us to find out the next topic.

Youth Sessions

When: Every Wednesday (term time)
Time: 4:00 pm – 6:00 pm
Where: Tottenham Green, Haringey, London

Support for young people: education, skills, life guidance. Open to ages 16–25.

Good to know: All our sessions are free. We speak English, French, Lingala and other languages. If you need a translator or have accessibility needs, please get in touch before you visit.
